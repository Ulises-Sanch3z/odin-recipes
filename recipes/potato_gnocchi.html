Potato Gnocchi

Description

Potato gnocchi are tender, fluffy, and easier to make than you may think! Four basic pantry ingredients and a light touch are all it takes to make this iconic Italian dish at home.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg red delight potato boiled skin on
  • 3 cup plain flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 pinch sal and pepper

Steps

  1. Peel and mash cooled potatoes and place in a large bowl.
  2. Add flour, egg, and salt and pepper to taste. Combine well.
  3. Knead lightly until dough is manageable and not too sticky.
  4. Add a little more flour if the dough is too moist.
  5. Break off a portion of dough and roll to 1 cm in thickness on a floured board. Cut into 3 cm lengths and indent each piece on a fork.
  6. Place on a floured tray until ready to use.
  7. Continue this process until all the dough is used up.
  8. Cook the gnocchi in batches in a large pot of salted boiling water.
  9. Remove with a slotted spoon as they rise to the top.
  10. Serve with plain tomato pasta sauce and parmesan cheese.
